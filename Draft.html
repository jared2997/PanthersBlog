Mock Draft 1.0


This is the first of many mock drafts I'll be doing for the Carolina Panthers for the 2024 NFL Draft.


2.33: WR Xavier Legette, South Carolina


Legette is a 6 foot 3 wide receiver from the University of South Carolina and was the WR1 for the Gamecocks this year. He ended the year with 71 receptions and had 1,255 yards with 7 touchdowns in 2023. He also is 227 pounds and is getting comparisons to DK Metcalf and he would be a good fit for this team who is still searching for a number 1 WR. Legette and Bryce I think would hopefully gain chemistry in the offseason and in training camp and hopefully become the next great QB/WR duo in the NFL for years to come.



3.65: C Zach Frazier, West Virginia


Frazier stands 6 foot 3 and weighs 310 pounds. He would start if the Panthers end up cutting Bradley Bozeman and he was a 4 year starter at West Virginia and was the first freshman since 1980 to start on the offensive line for the program. He is a very strong guy and can win battles upfront with defensive tackles, which is what the Panthers really need. And to get this guy in the 3rd round is great for the Panthers.



4.97: TE Cade Stover, Ohio State


Stover stands 6 foot 4 and weighs 251 pounds and is a great receiving threat for the Panthers. While Hayden Hurst was out, Tommy Tremble was the TE to start receiving even though he shines in blocking. Stover had 41 receptions for 576 yards and 5 touchdowns in 2023 and can bring that to the Panthers would use him to back up Hurst and Tremble.
